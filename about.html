Hi, I'm Ania!

I'm a computer science student at San Jose State University in California. I am passionate about combining bioengineering with computer science. I love expanding my horizons by learning new technologies and at the same time creating modern and innovative solutions.

In my free time, I travel the world and compete in the international West Coast Swing Championships! My passion to dance has inspired me to combine my expertise in bioengineering and motion capture with computer science knowledge. So far I have created two projects: Motion Capture Dance Analysis and Motion AI. While the first one involved using a specialized mocap equipment, the second was creating using OpenCv and MediaPipe in order to make it more accessible for wider audience. While I am still working on improving these projects, I also plan to expand their usage much further than just dance. I plan to incorporate the systems for rehabilitation and therapy.
